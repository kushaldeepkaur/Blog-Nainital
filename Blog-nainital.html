"Nainital: The Lake City"

A must visit hill station

Nainital, the beautiful hill station situated in the Kumaon region of India's Uttrakhand state.
It is one of the best tourism place with pleasant weather and climate for holidays. There are many attractions as: Nainital lake, Market, Ropeway, Tiffin top, Nainital zoo, Thandi sadak, religious spots i.e gurudwara ,Nanda devi temple, church and mosque , Naini peak, Snow view and many more...

City's mesmerising beauty attracts it's visitors for holiday and fun.
I think it's a must visit Himalayan resort town for holidays. And one must visit it!
